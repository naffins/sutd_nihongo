February is the month for romance and all things lovey dovey. To commemorate this special month and Valentine's Day, SUTD NiHonGo! decided to set up a booth made for couples to increase their "doki doki power" by participating in attractions.

Invite your special someone to play cooperative Taiko no Tatsujin with electronic drums modeled after traditional Taiko drums! Play casually for fun while singing to your favorite Anime, Vocaloid, or J-pop tunes, alternatively flex your rhythm game skills while bonding with your best friend.

We have also set up a special Photo Booth where couples have the opportunity to wear authentic Japanese kimonos, use emoji and love message as props, and bring home a picture of themselves complete with beautiful sakura blossoms and a traditional Japanese wooden frame as the backdrop.
